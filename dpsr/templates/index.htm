About Us


We, at X-Plore, provide travel services for our customers and strive to make their experience memorable. In these difficult times of the global pandemic, we provide a virtual touring service for our customers as well by connecting them with travel guides. We aim to serve our customers with a comfortable experience as well as solving the problem of unemployment in tour guides in these times.


Don't worry about phony travel guides. We verify our tour guides by taking voluntary reviews from our customers about their virtual tour.
